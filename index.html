About us

Welcome to Ink Tattoo!
We’re a custom tattoo studio & art gallery in United States of America.

Ink Tattoo Studio was established in 1970. Tattoos and Piercings are all offered in a clean, sterile, friendly environment. Feel free to come in with your own ideas and inspire our artists. We will also be happy to look over any reference materials you have to design the custom piece you desire.

We offer a variety of body piercings in a sterile environment. In addition to aftercare, we are available for jewelry changes and questions regarding existing body piercings.

Team

Corey Miller

Corey was born July 9, 1982. He was into painting and drawing since he was a child. Back then Corey Miller was a great inspiration. These days, he spends his spare time creating tattoo, abstract, and automotive related art. He started with Ink Tattoo Studio in June 2003. His favorite subject matter to tattoo is photorealistic black and grey, large scale Japanese motifs, and traditional themes. Corey is not fond of flash. On almost every occasion, he creates a custom piece for each client. He does this to preserve originality. Corey enjoys meeting new people and working with them to create that one-of-a-kind, perfect tattoo.

Kat Von D

Kat started tattooing at Ink Tattoo Studio in 2002. After tattooing for over a decade, Kat Von D has taken ownership of the business that has proudly been serving the community and surrounding area for over twenty years. Being intrigued by art and tattoos from an early age, she is easily inspired and is ready to discuss your ideas. Kat Von D will take the time to create the piece you will love to wear!
